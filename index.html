Why I am applying for the Course

"If you don’t know where you are going, you will probably end up somewhere else." –Lawrence J. Peter

My interest in computer science began years ago when I attended a technical engineering BootCamp with the Future Engineers Team. There, I learned about software development for the very first time. Since then, I have delved deeper into that field and spent a lot of my free time learning about it, I always thought of it as a hobby more than anything else. In 2021, I graduated from high school with an average of 98.7%, and I decided to study computer science.

Getting started with coding and getting to a good level without a guide was a challenge, so I took the coding foundation course offered by GSG. My team and I learned web development and created an amazing website together. I continued learning by myself after finishing the course to gain more experience and built other websites using new technologies.

Moreover, I believe my ability to solve problems helps me to stand up against difficult challenges, and participation in this academy will increase my knowledge and experience. Also, I am looking forward to making new friends, I have always wanted to be part of Gaza Sky Geeks since I strongly believe that being a part of such a community is something I will be proud of, and as a wise man once said: "he who takes no chances wins nothing", thus I am eager to take this opportunity that I really believe will help me in my career path, hoping that I will be accepted.
